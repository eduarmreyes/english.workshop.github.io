JEV Workshop English.

About usWe are a group of young passionate about teaching what God has allowed us to learn over time. To the Workshop you will enter as a student and you will leave it as a teacher.

MissionImparting vocational workshops and at the same time instructing you through the God's words at the worship; where each workshop will be offered by the servers of the ministry with the knowledge that each has in their respective area and thus began the work that goes along with what we have been preached in our church to spread the word by providing a "community service".

DescriptionLead to the feet of Christ to young people through vocational workshops.Providing young people a vocational workshop for attending church services. Train servers and trainers in the areas to teach and help young people in their learning while working with the ministry of Youth in Victory. Increase worship attendance through workshops.

Requirements:

  • Assist the Victory in Youth worship every Saturday at 9:00 am
  • Submit your record of attendance.
